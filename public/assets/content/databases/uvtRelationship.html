Databases and Information Retrieval Systems at UVT


West University of Timisoara has opportunities regarding Databases and IR


In second year at Computer Science (Romanian/English section) and Artificial Intelligence there is a Databases course with the general objective: "Understand and apply the relational database approach to design and implement systems and use cases from real-life."


There is also an opportunity to approach databases with a Master's Degree in Big Data (https://info.uvt.ro/programe-master/big-data/)
