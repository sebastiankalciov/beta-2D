Important people


Edgar F. Codd

Known as the father of relational databases.


Michael Stonebraker

Contributor to Ingres, PostgreSQL, and other database systems.


Jim Gray

Known for his work on database transaction processing and the development of the five properties of transactions (ACID). Winner of the Turing Award in 1998.


Jeffrey Ullman

Co-author of several foundational texts on database systems and algorithms. Winner of the Turing Award in 2020.


Jennifer Widom

Research in data management, data streams, and database education. Dean of the School of Engineering at Stanford.
