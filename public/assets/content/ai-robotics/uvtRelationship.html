Study programmes and research projects of interest for students that want to specialize in AI and Robotics:

  • Bachelor's programmes:
    • Computer Science: During the third year, students can choose between three study tracks, one of which is Artificial Intelligence. Some of the subjects offered are:
      • Intelligent Systems and Machine Learning,
      • Data Compression Algorithms,
      • Linear Algebra and Differential Equations,
      • Introduction to Neurotechnologies, etc.
    • Artificial Intelligence: During the third year, students can study subjects such as:
      • Machine Learning,
      • Neural Computing, Data Visualization and Analysis,
      • Autonomous Agents/Robot Process Automation,
      • Deep Learning,
      • Planning Techniques for Robotics or Natural Language Processing,
      • Computer Vision,
      • Human-AI interaction/Cognitive Robotics/Neuroscience, etc.
  • Master's programmes:
    • Artificial Intelligence and Distributed Computing: There are three study tracks within this programme, corresponding to the research areas students interested in completing a PhD could further pursue at UVT: AI, Distributed Systems, Computational Theory.
      Among the subjects studied are:
      • Advanced Logical and Functional Programming
      • Architectures for Parallel Computing
      • Operations Research and Optimization
      • Data Mining
      • Parallel Computing
      • Automated Theorem Proving
      • Computer Vision
      • Resource Management in Distributed and Parallel Systems
      • Meta-heuristic Algorithms
    • Big Data - Data Science, Analytics and Technologies: Focusing on Computational and Statistical Modelling and the Analysis of Large Volumes of Data, this programme offers subjects such as:
      • Probabilistic Models for Data Science
      • Data Analysis and Programming in R
      • Data Mining
      • Big Data Technologies
      • Biostatistics and Medical Data Analysis, etc.
    • Intelligent Software Robotics: Focusing on Automation, Software Robotics, Neurotechnologies, AI and Signal Processing, this programme offers subjects such as:
      • Computer Vision
      • Robotics Process Automation
      • Neuroscience - Brain Computing Interfaces, etc.
  • PhD programmes and Research:
    • Students can further pursue their interest in AI at UVT by completing a PhD programme. Examples of proposed thesis titles include:
      • Robotics and autonomous intelligent systems for Healthcare in Contemporary Society
      • Guiding optimization heuristics by using graph neural networks
      • Symbolic computation techniques for approximate reasoning
      • Tree automata techniques for regular language matching problems, etc.
    • Some of the areas on which the Research Center in Computer Science at UVT works are:
      • Big Data Applications and Data Analysis
      • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
      • Applications of Machine Learning in Earth Observation
      • Theory of Computing
      • Computation and Applied Mathematics
    • Current research projects at UVT include:
      • AiRobo (Artificial Intelligence based Robotics)
      • SAGE (Satisfiability checking, graph neural networks and symbolic computation)
      • EuroProofNet (European Network on Formal Proof)
      • ROTLA (Transliteration of 19th century Romanian texts written in the transitional alphabet using machine learning)
